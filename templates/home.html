What is CheckPoint?

CheckPoint is HackStreet’s ultimate 3-day hackathon — designed to bring together students, developers, and tech enthusiasts from around the UAE! Dive into a weekend of building projects, learning new tech, and teaming up with creative minds. Whether you’re a coding pro or just starting out, CheckPoint is all about innovation, collaboration, and having a blast with your teammates while solving real-world challenges.

When is CheckPoint?

Mark your calendars! CheckPoint will take place from September 5th to September 7th, 2025. Get ready for an intense weekend of coding and creation!

Who can attend CheckPoint?

CheckPoint is open to all students — no matter your major or skill level. Whether you're a seasoned coder, a creative designer, or just curious about tech, you're totally welcome to join! We love bringing together people from all kinds of backgrounds to create a fun, diverse, and collaborative space.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, laptop charger, and any other essential tech you might need. Don’t forget some money or snacks for food breaks. Most importantly, bring your enthusiasm and a willingness to learn!

Is there a cost to participate?

No, CheckPoint is absolutely FREE for all participants! We believe in making technology accessible to everyone, so the full experience — including workshops, mentorship, and swag — comes at no cost, thanks to our amazing sponsors. The only optional fee is for food: if you’d like us to handle meals for you during the event, you can chip in a small amount. Otherwise, feel free to bring your own or grab food your way!

Do I need a team?

You can register as an individual or as a team of up to 3 members. If you don’t have a team, no worries — many participants sign up solo, and you’ll have plenty of chances to connect with others during the event. We also encourage you to hop into our Discord server to meet fellow hackers, ask questions, and stay in the loop!

What kind of projects can I build?

You can build anything you can imagine — as long as it’s based on the theme: gamified productivity and street culture. Whether it’s a web app, mobile app, AI model, or hardware hack, your project should creatively incorporate those two keywords in any way you interpret them. Take the theme, run with it, and make something totally unique!
