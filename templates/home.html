Join us at CheckPoint!

Get ready for an electrifying event filled with innovation, collaboration, and challenges. Connect with like-minded individuals, expand your skills, and build amazing projects. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting, CheckPoint offers an unforgettable experience!

What is CheckPoint?

CheckPoint is an annual hackathon designed to bring together students, developers, and tech enthusiasts from around the globe. It's a 48-hour event where participants collaborate to build innovative solutions, learn new technologies, and network with mentors and industry professionals. Our goal is to foster creativity, problem-solving, and a passion for technology in a fun and challenging environment.

When is CheckPoint?

Mark your calendars! CheckPoint will take place from September 5th to September 7th, 2025. Get ready for an intense weekend of coding and creation!

Who can attend CheckPoint?

CheckPoint is open to all university students, regardless of their major or skill level. Whether you're a seasoned coder, a budding designer, or just curious about technology, you're welcome to join! We encourage participants from diverse backgrounds to foster a rich and collaborative environment.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, charger, and any essential tech accessories you might need. Most importantly, bring your enthusiasm and a willingness to learn!

Is there a cost to participate?

No, CheckPoint is absolutely FREE for all accepted participants! We believe in making technology accessible to everyone. Food, drinks, and swag will be provided throughout the event, thanks to our generous sponsors.

Do I need a team?

You can register as an individual or as a team of up to 3 members. If you don't have a team, don't worry! We'll have dedicated team-building sessions at the beginning of the event to help you connect with other participants and form a winning team.

What kind of projects can I build?

You can build anything you can imagine! Projects can range from web and mobile applications to hardware hacks, AI/ML models, and more. We encourage innovative solutions that address real-world problems. Challenges and themes will be announced at the opening ceremony to inspire your creativity.
